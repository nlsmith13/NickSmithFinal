What is Neurofeedback?

Neurofeedback, also known as EEG biofeedback, is a groundbreaking approach that uses real-time displays of brain activity to teach self-regulation of brain function. It is a non-invasive therapy with applications ranging from mental health to peak performance enhancement.

This cutting-edge technique has been embraced by mental health professionals, athletes, and individuals aiming to enhance their cognitive skills or manage conditions like ADHD, anxiety, and depression.

Understanding Neurofeedback

Neurofeedback is a non-invasive technique that uses real-time brain activity monitoring to help individuals improve their mental well-being.
