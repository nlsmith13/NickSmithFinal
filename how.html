The Neurofeedback Process

Sensors are placed on the scalp to monitor brain activity, which is then visualized on a screen. This feedback is used to guide the brain into more functional states through positive reinforcement.

With consistent practice, neurofeedback sessions train the brain to operate at an optimal level, offering significant improvements in behavior, mood, and cognitive abilities.

The Process

Neurofeedback works by reading brain signals and providing real-time positive reinforcement when the brain reaches desired states, training it to function optimally.
